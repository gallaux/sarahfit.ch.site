About me

Hi, I'm Sarah! My passion for lifting began over 10 years ago when I signed up for a 10-pack of personal training sessions at my local gym. After a few sessions, I built up the courage to ask my trainer to teach me the fundamentals of the big three: squat, deadlift, and bench press. I’ll never forget the excitement in his eyes when I asked, and how eager he was to guide me through each session that followed. I also can’t forget how much more capable and empowered I felt in the gym.

Since then, I've developed a love for all sorts of activities—everything from Jiu Jitsu and Crossfit to running half and full marathons, rock climbing, paddleboarding, and yoga. My life feels most in tune when I'm moving my body. This journey inspired me to become a certified personal trainer, so I can help others discover the joy of movement and empower them to reach their strongest, healthiest selves.

Resistance training is essential for living a long, vibrant life, and movement is my way of expressing gratitude to my body for all it’s given me. It’s time to say thank you to yours. Let’s begin your training journey today.

Personal training

Ready to take your fitness to the next level with expert, one-on-one coaching? In our personal training sessions, I’ll help you achieve your goals with a fully customized plan designed for your body, fitness level, and aspirations—whether you’re looking to build strength, improve endurance, lose weight, or feel more confident in your body.

But we don’t just focus on getting results. My top priority is ensuring that every movement is done with perfect form to prevent injury and maximize your progress. With hands-on guidance and constant form correction, I’ll teach you how to move properly and efficiently, setting you up for long-term success. Quality of movement is key—not just for avoiding injury but for building a strong foundation that supports all of your fitness goals.

With personalized attention, motivation, and accountability, you’ll make steady progress while staying safe and injury-free. Let’s work together to unlock your full potential—schedule your first session today and start moving smarter, safer, and stronger!
